Server Rules

  1. No hacking/cheating - Any use of hacked clients, exploits, or unfair advantages will result in a permanent ban with no exceptions.
  2. Be respectful - No racism, sexism, homophobia, or other forms of discrimination. Treat others how you want to be treated.
  3. No griefing - Destroying others' builds, stealing items, or any form of griefing is strictly prohibited.
  4. No spamming - This includes chat spam, command spam, sign spam, or any other disruptive behavior.
  5. No advertising - Do not promote other servers, websites, or services without staff permission.
  6. Use common sense - Staff have final say in all rule interpretations and situations not explicitly covered here.
  7. Keep it family-friendly - No inappropriate builds, skins, usernames, or chat content.
  8. No scamming - Scamming players out of items, currency, or services is not allowed.
  9. Respect staff decisions - Arguing with staff in public chat is not permitted. Use /msg for concerns.
